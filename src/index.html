Providing quality care for your small and large animals

For over 20 years, we have been providing the Central Hawkes Bay region with expert veterinarian advice delivered in a personal and caring way. With animal wellfare as our top priority, you can be reassured knowing that CHB Vets is the best place for your animals in their time of need.

We operate on a 24-hour, on-call service spanning 365 days a year so that we can be available to you for any emergency.
